By removing the hassle of physical business cards, we are bringing working professionals together and enabling a more personal and efficient networking experience.

How it works

Working professionals know what a hassle it is to keep track of business cards. Whether at networking events or in daily interactions, exchanging business cards can often be more trouble than it's worth. But now, with Meishi, all of your business cards are at the tip of your fingers—organized neatly into a single app.

Simply input your information into a customizable digital business card and no longer worry about keeping a stack of business cards on hand. With QR technology, every business card comes with it's own QR code to easily exchange with others.

All you have to do is scan a person's QR code and their business card will be automatically added to your deck, easily searchable and organized. Quick and easy — giving you more time to get down to business.
